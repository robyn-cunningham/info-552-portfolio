PROFESSIONAL PHILOSOPHY STATEMENT

Robyn serves Warner Library as the Reference and Outreach Librarian. She is currently completing her MLIS degree and will be graduating in the fall of 2023. In her free time, she creates art that she sells in the form of prints on her online shop, along with taking commission inquiries. She enjoys combining her two passions whenever possible.

Meet the Librarian

I am a academic librarian at Warner Library of Eastern University, and I create digital art using Procreate and Apple products. Some of my other hobbies and passions include mental health advocacy, reading, and watercolor painting.
